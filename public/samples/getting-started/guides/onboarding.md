# Onboarding

New hires start by reviewing the [remote work policy](../policies/remote-work.md)
and meeting their manager to agree on a first-week schedule.

## First-week checklist

- Confirm access to required systems.
- Review team communication norms.
- Read the policies relevant to your role.
- Schedule a 30-day check-in with your manager.
